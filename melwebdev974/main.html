Facts About Réunion Island

Réunion Island is a French overseas department located in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and unique history.

Geography

  • Réunion is home to one of the world’s most active volcanoes: Piton de la Fournaise.
  • The island has three major cirques: Mafate, Cilaos, and Salazie — ancient collapsed volcano craters turned into dramatic valleys.
